\# Journal Agent — Surface Vision



\## Surface Purpose

Journal is the platform’s memory and reflection surface.



It should answer:

\- What was I thinking before I took this trade?

\- What was the setup?

\- What were the targets and risks?

\- What actually happened?

\- What do I want to remember next time?

\- What context mattered?



\## Surface Structure

Journal should feel like:

\- a trading notebook

\- a structured timeline

\- a set of lightweight forms/checklists

\- a place for fast capture and later review



\## Surface Modes

Journal should support modes like:

\- quick note

\- trade idea

\- pre-trade worksheet

\- post-trade review

\- event timeline

\- filtered review



\## Relationship to Other Surfaces

\- Watchlist may send symbols or ideas into Journal

\- Scanner may send shortlisted candidates or search context

\- Artifact may send saved analysis context

\- Alerts may send triggered events

\- Positions may send prefilled trade context



Journal is where all of that gets compressed into something worth remembering.



\## User Goal

The user should feel:

“I can capture what matters quickly now, and still get value from it later.”
