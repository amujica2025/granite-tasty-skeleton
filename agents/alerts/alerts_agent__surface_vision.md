\# Alerts Agent — Surface Vision



\## Surface Purpose

Alerts is the platform’s signal-delivery and rule-management surface.



It should answer:

\- Can this platform notify me right now?

\- What rules are active?

\- What should I be alerted on?

\- Which alerts fired recently?

\- Can I turn rules on/off quickly?



\## Surface Structure

The Alerts surface should feel like:

\- a compact control panel

\- a notification reliability dashboard

\- a rule list

\- a quick-create surface



\## Surface Modes

Alerts should support modes like:

\- test delivery

\- active rules

\- create rule

\- edit rule

\- event history (optional)



\## Relationship to Other Surfaces

\- Watchlist may send symbol-prefilled alert creation

\- Scanner may send candidate-prefilled alert creation

\- Positions may send position-related alert creation

\- Artifact may later export an insight into an alert



Alerts should receive context, not own those surfaces.



\## User Goal

The user should feel:

“If something important happens, this app will tell me fast and reliably.”
